Gallery of Confectionery Decoration

This page is a small exhibited gallery of decorated confectionery in Victorian time from the 1862 "Francatelli's Royal Confectioner". With the improvement of manufacture and living quality in 19th century, confectionery decoration became new fashion to make food more delicate, not only eatable, but also enjoyable. 

The decoration illustrations also indicate different aesthetic taste of 19th century confectioners from today. For instance, our appetite wouldn't be raised by a boar head cake nowadays.
