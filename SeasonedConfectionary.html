「the suitability of certain foods to certain times of the year, and the pleasures of eating the vegetables, fruits, poultry, meat or fish which is in season, therefore at its best, most plentiful, and cheapest.  」

-- Elizabeth David

This page is to display bills of fare for confectionery in different seasons.Due to technical restriction of food storage and food preservation, seasoned recipes with fresh ingredients were once popular in 19th century. 

Elizabeth David also firmly believed in eating food in season; she loved "the pleasure of rediscovering each season's vegetables" and thought it "rather dull to eat the same food all year round".
